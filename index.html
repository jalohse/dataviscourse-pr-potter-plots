Potter Plots

The Project

Our project aims at exploring pieces from one of the best selling book series of all time, Harry Potter. Before this website, an interactive visualization that looked at different aspects of the books did not exist. With this implementation, it is now easy to see how certain characters interact and the spells that are used in each book. Not only can you view all of the books at one time, you can also view individual books to see the trends that appeared in each. Our process book explains how we gathered this data, how our design evolved, and how these visualizations were implemented.
